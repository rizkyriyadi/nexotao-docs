# Models & Pricing

All active models, grouped by modality, with rates in the relevant unit for each.
Pulled directly from `GET /models` — always in sync with the live catalog, with no
hardcoded prices.

<LivePricing />

## How to read the rates

- **Text** — rate per 1 million tokens, separately for input and output.
- **Image** — rate per megapixel; cost = (width × height / 1M) × rate × number of
  images.
- **Transcribe** — `gpt-4o` is billed per audio token + output text tokens;
  `whisper` per minute.

Billing details are in [Billing & Pricing](/penagihan).
